### Mod Installation Notes

- Subscribe to required mods via Steam Workshop.
- Open Arma 3 Launcher → Mods tab.
- Create and save a preset for your selected server.
- Optional mods may be enabled at your discretion.
- If you receive connection errors, verify required mods are active and no unsupported mods are loaded.
