How To Join

Four steps from zero to your first operation.

Join Discord

Discord is the operational hub — schedules, briefings, mod lists and announcements all live here. Start here.

Join Discord →

Direct Connect to Server

  1. Launch Arma 3 Launcher
  2. Navigate to Servers
  3. Select Direct Connect
  4. Enter Server IP + Port
  5. Connect

If you get a mod error, proceed to Step 3.

Install Required Mods

  1. Exit game
  2. Open Launcher → Mods tab
  3. Subscribe to required mods via Steam Workshop
  4. Build and save a preset for the server

Installation Instructions

  • Subscribe to required mods via the Steam Workshop links above.
  • Open Arma 3 Launcher → Mods tab and verify all mods are active.
  • Create and save a preset for your selected server.
  • Optional immersion mods are client-side — enable them at your discretion.
  • If you receive connection errors, verify all required mods are active and no unsupported mods are loaded.

Operations

Structure, doctrine and role guidance for Green Sharks operations.

Operation Structure

  • Work as a coordinated group at all times
  • Heal and support teammates in the field
  • Respect mission makers and Zeus guidance
  • Follow assigned roles and task assignments
  • Provide constructive feedback in designated channels

CAS Engagement Rules

All pilots must acknowledge CAS rules before flying. Verify all targets via map before engaging.
Weapon TypeMin Distance
Missile / Rocket strikes75 m
Bombs (standard)100 m
Napalm / Daisy Cutter150 m
  • Do not bomb HQ or mortars
  • Avoid striking close to FOB
  • Leave custom objectives to ground troops on event days
  • Deliver supplies when not conducting air support
  • Never leave men on ground during extract
  • Primary mission: support ground troops — not the scoreboard

RTO — Fire Support Timing

  • Supports may be called once per restart (some recharge)
  • 15 seconds minimum between requests
  • ~1 minute from call to impact
  • 25 seconds from "aircraft inbound" to impact

Role Overview

  • Ground infantry — primary combat element
  • CAS pilots — air support and supply runs
  • RTO — coordinates fire support calls
  • Zeus — mission maker and event director
  • Aircraft use requires roster or admin approval
  • Role assignments are respected for the full op

Rules

Firm but fair. Common sense and teamwork above all.

General Conduct

  • Respect admins and mission makers
  • No excessive explosives or spam mines
  • No abusing arsenals
  • No Fortnite-style building exploits
  • Do not intentionally block AI pathfinding
  • Use aircraft only if rostered or approved

Operation Days

  • Work as a coordinated unit
  • Actively heal and support teammates
  • Provide constructive feedback in designated channels
  • Respect custom objective structure
  • Follow Zeus and admin direction

CAS Rules (Summary)

  • Verify all targets on map before firing
  • Observe minimum strike distances
  • Do not bomb HQ, mortars, or FOB vicinity
  • Leave event objectives to ground troops
  • Support the ground — not the scoreboard
  • Maintain safe distance between aircraft

Community Expectations

  • Treat all players with respect
  • Constructive feedback only
  • International players are welcome
  • No toxicity, elitism or gatekeeping
  • Represent Green Sharks well in joint ops

FAQ

Common questions answered.

Do I need prior Arma experience? +
No experience required. We'll teach you the basics. Show up, follow direction, and you'll pick it up quickly.
Are there any fees or membership costs? +
No. Green Sharks is completely free to join and participate in. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no tiers.
Is this hardcore milsim? +
Structured and serious — but welcoming. We take our ops seriously without being elitist. Teamwork and communication are expected; rigid military cosplay is not.
Can I play from Australia or the US? +
Yes. We operate AU and US server clusters. The US server is also playable from AU at acceptable ping. Check Discord for current operation schedules across timezones.
Do I need all the optional mods? +
No. Only required mods are needed to connect and play. Optional immersion mods are client-side and run entirely at your discretion.
Where do I find the operation schedule? +
All operation schedules are posted in Discord. Join via the link and check the #op-schedule channel.
