Operations

Structure, doctrine and role guidance for Green Sharks. AU and US clusters.

AU Operations

Australian cluster: Vietnam Mike Force, WW2 Mike Force, Zeus ops, Reforger.

Mike Force — Vietnam & WW2

  • Large-scale PvE with S.O.G. Prairie Fire / Unsung (Vietnam) and Spearhead 1944 (WW2)
  • Structured CAS support and RTO coordination
  • Event schedule and briefs in Discord #op-schedule
  • Direct connect via server IP/port from the Servers page

Zeus Operations

  • Custom curated missions run by mission makers and admins
  • Objective-based, cooperative play
  • Respect Zeus and mission design; feedback in designated channels

Reforger — Modern & Vietnam PvE

  • AU/NZ Modern Conflict and Vietnam PvE servers
  • BattleMetrics links on the Servers page
  • Same community standards; join Discord for times

Check Discord for the current AU operation schedule and timezone-specific times.

Doctrine & Roles

Operation structure, CAS rules and RTO timing.

Operation Structure

  • Work as a coordinated group at all times
  • Heal and support teammates in the field
  • Respect mission makers and Zeus guidance
  • Follow assigned roles and task assignments
  • Provide constructive feedback in designated channels

CAS Engagement Rules

All pilots must acknowledge CAS rules before flying. Verify all targets via map before engaging.
Weapon TypeMin Distance
Missile / Rocket strikes75 m
Bombs (standard)100 m
Napalm / Daisy Cutter150 m
  • Do not bomb HQ or mortars
  • Avoid striking close to FOB
  • Leave custom objectives to ground troops on event days
  • Deliver supplies when not conducting air support
  • Never leave men on ground during extract
  • Primary mission: support ground troops — not the scoreboard

RTO — Fire Support Timing

  • Supports may be called once per restart (some recharge)
  • 15 seconds minimum between requests
  • ~1 minute from call to impact
  • 25 seconds from "aircraft inbound" to impact
Strike TypeCooldown
Commando Vault (Daisy Cutter)30 min

F4 Bomb Strike5.5 min
Napalm Strike5.5 min
20mm Gun Run2.5 min
Illumination5 min

Role Overview

  • Ground infantry — primary combat element
  • CAS pilots — air support and supply runs
  • RTO — coordinates fire support calls
  • Zeus — mission maker and event director
  • Aircraft use requires roster or admin approval
  • Role assignments are respected for the full op
